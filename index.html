About Me

My name is Ryan Lavin. As an artist myself for many years, I realise the importance of a good frame in taking a piece to the next level.

A little bit more about myself, I was born in New York before being reared here in Ballinderreen, Co.Galway. I lived here on the Wild Atlantic Way (before it was called that) until the age of eighteen. And after some time in the US playing music, I have returned and taken up framing, alongside my art, as another part of my creative works.
